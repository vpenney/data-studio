Some states do have weird beer laws to watch out for, including the twelve that still uphold the Prohibition-era Blue Law that bans liquor sales on Sunday. Alabama, Indiana, Minnesota, Mississippi, Montana, North Carolina, Oklahoma, South Carolina, Tennessee, Texas, Utal, and West Virginia are the ones to watch out for.

Also, if you order a beer in Utah, you have to also order food, but good luck finding somewhere to drink in that state anyway.
